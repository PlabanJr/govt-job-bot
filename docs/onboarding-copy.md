# WhatsApp Onboarding Copy

## English

Consent:
"Do you want central government job alerts on WhatsApp? Reply YES to opt in. You can reply STOP anytime to opt out."

Language:
"Choose language: Reply 1 for English, 2 for हिंदी."

Frequency:
"How often do you want alerts? Reply 1 for Daily, 2 for Weekly."

Profile - Name:
"Please share your full name."

Profile - Date of Birth:
"Please share your date of birth (DD-MM-YYYY)."

Profile - Highest Qualification:
"What is your highest qualification? (e.g., 12th, Diploma, B.A., B.Tech, M.A., M.Sc)"

Profile - Category:
"Select your category: GEN, EWS, OBC, SC, ST."

Profile - PwD:
"Are you a person with disability (PwD)? Reply YES or NO."

Profile - Ex-Serviceman:
"Are you an ex-serviceman? Reply YES or NO."

Profile - Gender (optional):
"Share your gender (optional). Reply SKIP to skip."

Profile - Location (optional):
"Share your location preference (optional). Reply SKIP to skip."

Confirmation:
"Thanks! You are subscribed to {frequency} central government job alerts in {language}. We only send official sources."

Preferences:
"Reply PREFERENCES anytime to update language, frequency, or profile."

Opt-out:
"You have been opted out. Reply START to re-subscribe."
